The IEEE WIE (Women in Engineering) group at An-Najah National University empowers and supports women in engineering and technology. Through workshops, mentorship, and networking events, WIE helps students develop technical and leadership skills, connect with role models, and contribute to innovations. WIE stands for Women in Engineering, reflecting the group’s dedication to promoting diversity and advancing women in the field.

Take Control of Your Time

We organized a valuable session with Ms. Clara Yaish, “Take Control of Your Time,” guiding students on effective time management, study techniques, stress and anxiety management, and maintaining mental well-being—helping them prepare for exams productively while actively engaging in an insightful and practical experience.

Women's Day gifts

As part of WIE community activities for International Women’s Day, we gifted a simple yet meaningful token—a rose-shaped candle—to the Faculty of Computer & Electrical Engineering and the Deanship of Student Affairs, celebrating the creativity and achievements of women and honoring their contributions.
