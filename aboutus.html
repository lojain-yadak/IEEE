Why choose Us

Because learning doesn’t happen alone. Our goals are simple:-

Enhancing collaboration among students from different majors:

Creating an environment that encourages knowledge sharing between students across various engineering and technology fields.

Raising awareness of the importance of technology through practical methods:

Hosting discussion sessions and presentations that explain the role of engineering and technology in everyday life.

Empowering students with professional development opportunities suited to their abilities:

Offering short trainings, career-guidance sessions, and networking opportunities that support their future paths.

Encouraging innovation through simple and effective activities:

Organizing small competitions and hands-on workshops to help students develop their research and development skills.

Our Approach

Who We Are & What We Stand For

our mission

We strive to advance technological innovation and scientific excellence to serve humanity. We focus on developing creative engineering solutions that improve quality of life and drive scientific and technological progress across all fields.

our vision

We believe that technology is the key to a brighter future. That’s why we aim to empower students to become part of that future by building an inspiring tech-driven community where they collaborate to create innovative ideas that positively impact their lives and surroundings. Through educational and hands-on opportunities, we aspire to become a leading source of knowledge and progress, contributing to both local and global improvement through inventive technological solutions.

our value

Academic excellence through strengthening technical mastery, innovation by encouraging creative solutions, collaboration through partnerships and teamwork, and social responsibility by applying knowledge to improve lives.

Common IEEE Questions and Answers

Any student studying a field related to IEEE, such as various branches of engineering, computer science, information technology, and others, is eligible to apply for membership.

The registration opens during the first few weeks of each semester, and the exact dates are announced through social media platforms.

To join the IEEE chapter at An-Najah National University, you must first register by clicking the "Become a Member" button in the link containing the membership form (usually via Google Forms) and fill in the required information. After that, wait for the announcement of payment dates and locations through our social media platforms. Once the locations and times are announced, go to the specified place at the scheduled time to pay the fees and confirm your membership. After completing the payment, you will receive an email with all the information you need to access the main website and benefit from its services.

To renew your membership in the IEEE chapter at An-Najah National University, first click the "Renew Your Membership" button in the membership renewal link (usually via Google Forms) and fill in the required information. Then, wait for the announcement of payment dates and locations through our social media platforms. Once the locations and times are announced, go to the specified place at the scheduled time to pay the fees needed to renew your membership. After completing the payment, you will receive an email containing all the information you need to maintain your membership on the website and continue enjoying its services.
