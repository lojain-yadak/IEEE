The IEEE RAS (Robotics and Automation Society) group at An-Najah National University supports students in robotics, automation, and related fields. Through workshops, seminars, hands-on courses like Arduino, competitions, and networking, RAS helps members enhance their technical skills, collaborate on real projects, and explore new ideas in robotics. RAS stands for Robotics and Automation Society, highlighting its commitment to advancing technology in the field.

RASCADIA

We participated in the RAS Student Chapters Bootcamp, alongside students from Jordan, Egypt, Tunisia, and Kenya, for two weeks of hands-on tech workshops. Arduino V10 – New & Improved

We successfully concluded the Arduino course, a 42-hour training program where students learned the basics of programming, electronic components, and how they work. The course offered an engaging and creative learning experience, culminating in a recognition ceremony honoring both participants and volunteers for their outstanding contributions. This hands-on course marked an inspiring step toward a future full of innovation and creativity.

Robotics in graduation projects: From idea to completion

We hosted an exceptional session for students working on hardware graduation projects and anyone passionate about robotics.
