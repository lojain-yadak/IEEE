Welcome to IEEE An-Najah National University

The IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) branch at An-Najah National University is a vibrant student community that fosters innovation, collaboration, and professional growth. As part of the world's largest technical professional organization, the branch empowers students in the fields of electrical engineering, computer science, and related disciplines to excel in their academic and professional journeys. Through workshops, seminars, competitions, and networking events, the IEEE branch at An-Najah provides a platform for students to connect with industry leaders, enhance their technical skills, and contribute to technological advancements that shape the future. The acronym IEEE stands for the (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) symbolizing the organization's dedication to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.
